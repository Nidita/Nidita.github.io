Our Memories With Food!

I don’t know about all, I can connect food with all the memories from my childhood to now. I can start from beginning. My mom used to spend her whole day for feeding me only some spoons of hotchpotch as I had a bitter feeling on having foods. I grew up and started to go to school. The tiffin-box that my mom provided me in primary-school days seemed to me like surprise box. When I was in high school I was really keen to know about the mid-day meal of school and eagerly waiting for going home to have the delicious lunch with my family. The enjoyable birthday parties with lot of delicious foods and friends, going to restaurant for achieving exam goals are really precious moments of my life. The festival days with my grandma and her heavenly cooking are actually out of world. Even my vacations with my cousins become more enjoyable with foods. I know, I have complained to my mother a lot about her cooking. Now in university life, I miss her cooking most. When I come home, that divine ambrosia gives me a touch of happiness and care. Nowadays I try to cook for my family, take the recipes from my mom and help my dad in his cooking experiments. Food is emotion, food is life.

~Nidita Roy
